We strive for producing Mechanical Engineering professionals having sound technical knowledge with innovative approach, who are competent to pursue diverse and successful careers in modern society. Through excellence in teaching, improvement in quality of life and fulfillment of social needs.
Mission:
  1. To enhance the overall academic performance of the students gradually, thereby increasing their placement potential
  2. To provide state-of-the-art infrastructure to students in order to impart quality education in Mechanical Engineering.
  3. To develop the students for facing intellectual and ethical career challenges after graduation.
  4. To develop students with knowledge, attitude and skill of employability, Entrepreneurship (Be Job creator then job seekers) and professionally ethical citizens…
  5. To motivate staff and students for creative thinking, thereby enriching the teaching-learning experience and developing the research activity.
  6. To develop the Department of Mechanical Engineering as a centre of Excellence in Production technology and ‘Thermal Engineering’ in the next 10 years.
  7. To development students possessing strong knowledge base who will cater industry Academic in Various fields of Mechanical Engg. as highly qualified, skillful and motivated manpower.
  8. To educate the students for effective technical communication and teamwork.
  9. To prepare professionally competent and broadly educated Mechanical Engineers by developing their analytical abilities, encouraging culture of continuous learning and by adopting new technologies to solve complex problems.
